Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just Google Translate with markup?

No. AI does the initial translation (GPT-4 + DeepL, not Google). I then spend 4-6 hours per project adapting cultural references, checking gaming terminology against Spanish wikis/communities, and optimizing for conversion. You're paying for expertise, not automation.

Why is this cheaper than agencies?

Agencies have overhead (offices, account managers, multiple review rounds). I'm a one-person operation using AI to handle repetitive work, so I can focus on what actually matters: making your store page convert Spanish-speaking players.

What if my game uses technical/niche terminology?

That's exactly why you need expert QA. I cross-reference terms with Spanish gaming communities (Reddit, Discord, wikis) to ensure accuracy. If your game has custom terminology, we establish it together during onboarding.

How do I know the Spanish version will convert?

I offer a conversion-focused guarantee: If your Spanish store page doesn't maintain at least 80% of your English conversion rate after 30 days (minimum 1K impressions), I'll rewrite it for free.

Do you translate in-game text / UI?

Not currently. I focus on marketing materials (store pages, announcements, press kits). For full game localization, I can refer you to specialized studios.

What if I need revisions?

All packages include revision rounds (1-3 depending on tier). Most clients use 0-1 revisions because the onboarding call ensures I understand your game's tone upfront.
